What Happened on February 18, 1984

Historical Events

  • East German figure skater Katarina Witt wins first of 2 consecutive women's singles gold medals at the Sarajevo Winter Olympics; also wins gold in Calgary (1988)
  • Finnish cross-country skier Marja-Liisa Hämäläinen wins the 20k gold medal at the Sarajevo Winter Olympics; sweeps all 3 individual events, also winning 5k and 10k events

Olympic Gold

Finnish ski jumper Matti Nykänen wins first of 4 career Olympic gold medals in the Large Hill individual event at the Sarajevo Winter Games

  • Revised concordat between Italy & Vatican signed

Famous Birthdays

  • Buddy Nielsen, American singer (Senses Fail), born in Ridgewood, New Jersey
