Miley Cyrus just announced that she will be performing in the Super Bowl LV NFL pregame event for healthcare workers on February 7. The event will air on Tiktok and TV CBS. Miley Cyrus Wears Sexiest Outfits Yet In New Instagram Posts

Miley posed for pictures in a sexy latex one-piece bodysuit. Health care workers from Tampa and around the country!” the 28-year-old wrote on Instagram. Miley held a pair of pink pompoms and posed for the camera as fans are full of excitement ahead of her big performance.

She also stepped out for a quick grocery run in Los Angeles wearing a pair of grey leggings and a plain white tank top. The tank top was completely see-through and Miley went braless which sent the internet into a frenzy. She put her blonde locks in a bun and wore a nude-colored face mask amid the coronavirus pandemic. This look is definitely a bit more toned down than the previous looks she has been posting on Instagram.

Miley also wore a pair of yellow-tinted sunglasses on her head along with a simple pair of black Nike sneakers. Who cares if she doesn’t wear a bra? She has never been shy about wearing what she wants or caring what the haters think.

Miley just recently posted a picture walking down the street and lifting her shirt up to flash the camera. The “Plastic Hearts” Singer wore simple high heals and jeans along with a pair of black sunglasses.
