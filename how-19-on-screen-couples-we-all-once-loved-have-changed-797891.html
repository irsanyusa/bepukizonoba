How 19 On-Screen Couples We All Once Loved Have Changed

Not very long ago, we used to hurry home after a day at school or work to watch the latest episode of our favorite TV show. Soap operas plunged us into a world where feelings were more intense than an earthquake. There’s no surprise that we felt that the characters were almost our relatives, and we used hundreds of nerve cells worrying about their romantic relationships.

The Bright Side editorial team brushed away a nostalgic tear and found out what the actors look like today, whose romantic relationships on screen used to occupy our entire imagination.
