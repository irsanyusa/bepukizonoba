Shawn Mendes Spotted Shirtless With Alleged New Girlfriend

Shawn Mendes recently gone to the beach with alleged new girlfriend, Charlie Travers. The Canadian singer-songwriter was spotted stripping down with the Big Brother UK alum for an impromptu sunbathing date.

Shawn Mendes, Alleged New Girlfriend Strip Down to Underwear for Cozy Beach Date

Last Wednesday, Mendes and Travers were photographed enjoying the beach in Malibu, California. The pair appeared to have spent an impromptu beach date, relaxing in the sand, sharing some hugs, and casually talking to each other.

As per the photos obtained by TMZ, the “Treat You Better” singer stripped down to his black Calvin Klein underwear during the outing. Travers also went shirtless, leaving only her black bra top and lacy thong. She also accessorized her beach outfit with some silver earrings and an arm cuff.

After their casual sunbathing trip, the rumored couple left the beach in jeans. They were also holding their sweaters while exiting the place.

Mendes and Travers first made headlines when they were spotted dining together at a restaurant in West Hollywood. At the time, Travers was seen putting her arms around the singer.

The pair’s possible brewing romance comes after Mendes’ breakup from Camila Cabello. He and the former The Voice coach began dating in July 2019 and quarantined together during the pandemic. Unfortunately, they called it quits in November 2021.

Earlier this year, news broke that the estranged couple might have possibly rekindled their relationship after they were caught locking lips at Coachella. Nonetheless, nothing was ever confirmed by the two. Be that as it may, it seems that Mendes has gotten over the “Havana” hitmaker.

Who is Charlie Travers?

Hailing from United Kingdom, Travers served as a host for several programs such as Grammy web series On Location and the Permission Slip Podcast. Furthermore, she appeared on the reality series Hollywood Medium as Tyler Henry’s assistant. She seemingly has a close relationship with Henry, being by his side for red carpet appearances. Travers and Henrys have also shared adorable tributes to each other on their social media.

In June 2013, she appeared in Big Brother season 14in the UK alongside her mother Jackie. As per The Sun, the pair was the show’s first-ever mother-daughter duo. Travers finished the series in fifth place.

During her time on the reality show, she had a relationship with her fellow contestant Dexter Koh. According to Digital Spy, Koh called Travers his “soul mate” in the show’s Diary Room and said he’d like to marry her. Unluckily, their love was not successful.
