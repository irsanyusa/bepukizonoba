The Masked Singer host Nick Cannon confirmed that he and Alyssa Scott are expecting another baby together on Thursday, as the pair posed for photos showing off Scott’s growing bump. This will be either Cannon’s eleventh or twelfth child.

Nick Cannon, Alyssa Scott Pose for Nude Pregnancy Photos

In October, Scott announced that she was pregnant, sharing photos of her baby bump while holding her daughter Zeela. It was reported that Cannon was the father of this new baby, and now the pair has confirmed it with a new photo shoot by photographer Gabriel Villalobos.

On Thursday, Scott posted a photo of herself standing up naked in a bathtub, covering her breasts with her hands. Cannon sits shirtless in the tub below her, touching her belly with one hand and her thigh with the other.

Scott also posted a black and white shot in which she sits in the tub with a towel wrapped around her hair. Cannon sits in front of her, kissing her belly. An artist named Glory also painted a version of the first photo on a canvas. Scott wrote that it would “forever be my favorite piece of artwork.”

The Pair Lost Son Zen Last Year

This will be Cannon and Scott’s second baby together. Last year, they welcomed a son named Zen, who sadly passed away in December of brain cancer. He was five months old. Cannon announced Zen’s death on his talk show, saying he “had the most beautiful spirit.”

In total, Nick Cannon has had ten children with six different women. This year alone, he has already welcomed three children with three women. In June, Bre Tiesi gave birth to their son Legendary Love. Then in September, Cannon welcomed daughter Onyx Ice with LaNisha Cole and son Rise Messiah with Brittany Bell.

Cannon is also currently expecting his third child with Abby De La Rosa. The pair already has twin sons named Zillion and Zion. Last month, they shared that they had been on a babymoon together ahead of this child’s birth.
