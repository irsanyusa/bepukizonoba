Priyanka Chopra Opened Up About Her Unhealthy Relationship Patterns Before Meeting Husband Nick Jona

Priyanka Chopra Jonas recently appeared on a podcast where she opened up about her romantic bond with her spouse, Nick Jonas. The 40-year-old actress reflected on the early stages of their relationship and shared some details about a “tumultuous relationship” that occurred before their marriage in 2018.

She was caught in a chaotic relationship prior to meeting Nick Jonas.

During the podcast, Priyanka revealed that in 2016, she was in the middle of a chaotic relationship. At the time, she had mutual friends with Nick, who advised her against continuing in that relationship and informed her that Nick was single. Although she was initially hesitant to engage in conversation with him, she eventually connected with Nick on Twitter.

At first, Chopra hesitated to connect with Jonas.

Priyanka shared that, at the time, she was 35 years old while Nick was 25. Initially, she was looking for a serious relationship and felt it was time to settle down.

Despite keeping in touch through “random texts,” Priyanka had reservations about entering into a relationship with Nick due to her past experiences with narcissistic ex-partners. Having experienced the thrills of dating, she felt ready to pursue a serious relationship. As a result, she put a stop to any potential fun and games between them.

Priyanka and Nick’s first time meeting was only a week before they attended the 2017 Met Gala as Ralph Lauren’s guests. Despite keeping in touch through “random texts,” Priyanka was still hesitant. She recognized a pattern in her relationships where she felt gaslit, putting her partners’ needs before her own. “I kept making the same mistakes,” the actress said.

With Nick, she found out what she needs in a partner.

Despite her initial doubts, Priyanka recognized that her relationship with Nick provided the clarity she needed to identify what she required in a partner. Years later, they welcomed their daughter, Malti Marie, on January 15, 2022. Malti spent over 100 days in the neonatal intensive care unit before finally coming home in time for Mother’s Day.

They embarked on the journey of parenthood together.

Reflecting on their journey in May 2022, Nick shared on Instagram, “Our baby is truly a badass... Our next chapter begins now.”

Moreover, an insider confirmed that the couple was ready for the challenge of parenthood and that it was something they had both wanted. Another source expressed happiness for the couple, saying that this was their time and that they were more than ready to embark on this new journey together.

In conclusion, Priyanka Chopra Jonas’ podcast offered a fresh perspective on her relationship with Nick Jonas. Despite some initial doubts and reservations, their love story has blossomed into a beautiful family with the arrival of their daughter.
