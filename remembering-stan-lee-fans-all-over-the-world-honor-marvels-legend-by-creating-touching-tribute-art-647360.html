Remembering Stan Lee: Fans All Over the World Honor Marvels Legend byCreating Touching Tribute

Stan Lee was a comic book writer, screenwriter, producer, and a man with unlimited imagination. The creator of the Marvel Universe passed away on November 12, 2018. He created more than 100 legendary superheroes including The Incredible Hulk, Thor, Spiderman, Doctor Strange, Iron Man, and Captain America.

Stan Lee made this world a better place thanks to the strength, kindness, and love that are invariably present in all of his stories and characters. He made many people believe that there were actual heroes in this world and amazing things could happen. Marvel fans around the world are reflecting back with great sadness on the passing of Stan Lee. Many of them have created touching art to praise his memory and work.
