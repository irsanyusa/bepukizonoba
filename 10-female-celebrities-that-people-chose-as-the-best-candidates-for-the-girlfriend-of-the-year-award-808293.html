10 Female Celebrities That People Chose as the Best Candidates for the Girlfriend of the Year

Some time ago, Ranker.com created a poll where people were asked to choose the best celebrity candidates for the “Girlfriend of the Year” award. While choosing the best of the best, the voters took into account not only the talent and professional skills of the nominees, but their personal qualities as well. Jennifer Lopez, Zendaya, Kristen Bell, Dua Lipa, and many other celebrities that we adore made it to the list, and the winner truly surprised us.

We at Bright Side gathered the voting results and here are the gorgeous female celebrities that people just can’t get enough in 2022.

10th place: Jennifer Lopez

VALERIE MACON/AFP/East News

Why people voted for Jennifer:

  • She is a talented actress, singer, and dancer
  • She looks even younger as she ages and she is the ultimate inspiration for many people when it comes to healthy living

9th place: Billie Eilish

Invision/Invision/East News

Why people voted for Billie:

  • She has won 7 Grammy Awards so far
  • She is the “bad guy” and people love her for that

8th place: Florence Pugh

Hahn Lionel/ABACA/Abaca/East News

Why people voted for Florence:

  • She is a talented actress
  • On top of that, she is also a wonderful singer

7th place: Taylor Swift

ANGELA WEISS/AFP/East News

Why people voted for Taylor:

  • She is known as an incredibly generous celebrity
  • She has won over 10 Grammy Awards so far

6th place: Jamie Chung

JEAN-BAPTISTE LACROIX/AFP/East News

Why people voted for Jamie:

  • She’s continued mastering her acting skills from one movie to another
  • She has successfully turned from a reality TV star to a professional actress

5th place: Dua Lipa

Marechal Aurore/ABACA/Abaca/East News

Why people voted for Dua:

  • She is gorgeous inside and out
  • People simply fell in love with her Future Nostalgia album

4th place: Selena Gomez

MICHAEL TRAN/AFP/East News

Why people voted for Selena:

  • She has a fantastic voice that people compare to the “voice of an angel”
  • She loves cooking

3rd place: Kristen Bell

Invision/Invision/East News

Why people voted for Kristen:

  • She’s played so many characters that people adore
  • She is an animal lover

2nd place: Zendaya

Invision/Invision/East News

Why people voted for Zendaya:

  • Apart from being a good actress, she is an incredible singer and dancer
  • She is also an animal lover

1st place: Margot Robbie

Jordan Strauss/Invision/AP/East News

Why people voted for Margot:

  • People find her super relatable in her interviews
  • She always looks stunning and has a great fashion sense
