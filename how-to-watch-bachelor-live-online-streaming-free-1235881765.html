“The Bachelor” finale officially airs tonight, during which bachelor Joey Graziadei will make his ultimate decision between Kelsey and Daisy, and as ABC as teased all season long, boast a shocking ending that’s never been seen before in the long-running franchise.

Like the episdoes before it, the finale will broadcast on ABC on Monday night at 9 p.m. ET. However, cable-cutters can stream the show live through a variety of live TV streamers such as DirecTV Stream, Sling TV, Fubo TV and Hulu + Live TV. The finale will also become available to stream the following day on Hulu, which is also home to all 28 seasons of the show.
