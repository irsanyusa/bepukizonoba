Dylan Sprouse, Barbara Palvin Are Reportedly Getting Married

After We Collided actor Dylan Sprouse is allegedly tying the knot with long-time girlfriend and model Barbara Palvin. An unknown source claimed that the couple has been engaged for months, they just haven’t publicized it.

Dylan Sprouse is Allegedly Engaged to Barbara Palvin

Wedding bells might soon be ringing for the actor and the supermodel. Back in early March, Sprouse and Palvin sparked engagement rumors when people spotted the Victoria’s Secret angel wearing a diamond on her finger during the Mammoth Film Festival. Nonetheless, while guts of their fans are strong, the two didn’t confirm anything about the speculations.

Today though, an unknown source claimed that Sprouse and Palvin have indeed taken their relationship to the next level. According to them, the two have been smitten with each other, especially now that they’re reportedly engaged.

“Dylan and Barbara are engaged,” the source said. “They have been showing off her ring to people and are so happy. They’re always very in love with one another and by each other’s sides, but they’re even happier than usual. They can’t wait to start this new chapter together.”

Furthermore, the source mentioned that the glow of the couple back in the Mammoth Film Festival suggests their engagement. Noting that Palvin at the time was a “social butterfly,” they said that the two would try to check in with each other.

As of the moment, neither Sprouse nor Palvin has responded to the ongoing assumptions. Be that as it may, it seems that their relationship is in a happy and contented state.

Their Romance Had a Slow Start

The Suite Life of Zack & Cody alum and the Hungarian supermodel have been together for approximately five years. Their romance kicked off back in 2017 when Sprouse met her at a party. When Palvin followed Sprouse on Instagram, the actor immediately sent her a dm.

“I was like, ‘Hey, I don’t know if you’re in New York for very long, but we should hang out if you want to. Here’s my number,'” he said.

Palvin, however, didn’t respond to Sprouse’s message. Apparently, it took her six months to text and reconnect with him. The rest, as they say, is history. They formally began dating in June 2018 and now, if the rumors are true, the two are ready to lead their romance to the altar.
