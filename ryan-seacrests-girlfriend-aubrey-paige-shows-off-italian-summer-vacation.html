Ryan Seacrest and girlfriend Aubrey Paige recently enjoyed a cozy getaway together in Italy. For those who may not know, the 48-year-old television host has been dating the 25-year-old model since 2021.

Aubrey Paige Shares Romantic Vacation Snaps with Ryan Seacrest

This summer, the lovebirds treated themselves to a European trip and captured their special moments on camera. The New York-based model gave a glimpse of their getaway via Instagram, sharing candid photos of their romantic experience.

“A summer of street markets, road trips, local artists and movie nights,” she wrote.

In the series of snapshots, Paige showed a picture of them together as they pose outside a lavish villa. Seacrest looked dashing in a navy linen shirt and beige slacks, wearing a huge boyish smile on his face. For her part, Pauge sported a patterned maxi dress and slipped on a black headband to polish her wavy hair.

The visual series also included a local art gallery, a picnic setup, and a video their Disney movie night. To cap off the post, Paige shared a photo of the American Idol host driving with a coffee in his hand.

It’s not the first time that Paige shared a glance of her bonding moments with Seacrest. Since they made their red carpet debut in 2022, the couple has been showing some of their PDA pics, from Seacrest’s Valentine’s Day surprises to their casual dates.

Fans Think He Still Hasn’t Gotten Over Ex-Girlfriend Shayna Taylor

While some fans were happy to see the couple enjoying their getaway, others became critical of the location of their vacation. For those who may not know, Seacrest’s ex-girlfriend of 10 years, Shayna Taylor is now living in Italy. This has apparently made one fan speculate that Seacrest is still hung up on Taylor.

“Really confused why you’re both in Italy so much. His ex moved there and now it seems he can’t stay away from Italy. Makes ya think,” one person wrote.

“Italy is huge! Are you running into your ex every day?” another user commented.

Since Seacrest and Paige went public with their relationship, many have shared their opinion about the couple. Over the years, many have pointed out their wide age gap. Apparently, some fans are not liking the fact that Seacrest is having a relationship with a woman who’s 23 years younger than him.

Be that as it may, Seacrest and Paige didn’t hold back and have celebrated their love on and off the spotlight.
