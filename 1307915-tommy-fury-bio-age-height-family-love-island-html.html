Boxing career

Has Tommy Fury won any fights? The young Tyson Fury brother, also known by his nickname, TNT, had 12 amateur fights before transitioning to the light-heavyweight category after signing a promotional deal with Fran Warren.

He has so far proved his doubters wrong since making his debut as a professional boxer, with three victories from his opening three bouts.

Tommy made his professional debut against Latvian Jevgenijs Andrejevs Jevgenijs, who was twice his age, on 22 December 2018 at the Manchester Arena, Manchester. The Latvian had suffered 100 defeats out of 119 bouts before TNT knocked him out.

Three months later, he faced fellow British boxer Callum Ide at Leicester Arena. Tommy knocked him out in the first round to hand him his 28th defeat in 30 fights.

The third and latest Tommy Fury fight was against Przemyslaw Binienda of Poland. The bout took place at Copper Box Arena, Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park, Hackney Wick, on Dec 21, 2019. It took him just 62 seconds to knock out the Polish international, who entered the bout with 26 defeats from 28 fights.
