Photos From Solange Knowles' Retro-Style Wedding In New Orleans

The 28-year-old bride rocked a chic Humberto Leon for Kenzo floor-length white gown with an attached cape, Jill for Lady Grey bracelets, and Stuart Weitzman shoes; while her 51-year-old husband was decked in a white Lanvin suit and black shoes.

Guests including big sister, Beyonce and her family, Jay Z and Blue Ivy, as well as the bride's mother, Tina Knowles, all turned up for the white-themed ceremony.

After the nuptials, Solange dressed in a daring low-cut jumpsuit alongside her husband in their usual retro style, rode on bicycles as the left the venue of the wedding.

While the bride danced joyfully during the wedding party, she suffered a minor wardrobe malfunction as her large cut-out onesie revealed a little too much while she danced the night away in a Mardi Gras style parade in New Orleans.

Her hairstyle was in its signature Afro and she added a pop of colour to her simple look with crimson lipstick that remained throughout the day.

Beyonce as usual looked very stylish in a long white dress, which sported spaghetti straps and a low-cut neckline. She added white platform heels, and wore her highlighted hair in voluminous curls.

The singer's adorable daughter, Blue Ivy, was dressed in a sparkling biege dress, while her husband, Jay Z rocked an all-white suit and shoes for the ceremony.

Solange's 10-year-old son, Daniel Julez Smith was also pictured with the groom before the ceremony as they arrived for the pre-wedding proceedings.
