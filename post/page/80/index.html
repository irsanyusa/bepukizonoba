KADIRS seals 4 Kafanchan hotels over 16.8 million unpaid taxes.

published on August 17, 2024
The Chairman of KADIRS, Jerry Adams, disclosed this while briefing newsmen after the operations on Thursday in Kaduna. He said the service carried out the operations on Wednesday in Kafanchan where it sealed four hotels, namely; Kyus Hotel, Sunshine Lodge, Bayan Hotel and Cloud-9 Lounge, over alleged tax defaults. Adams explained that the exercise was in line with the powers vested in the service under Section 104 (1) and (4) of the Personal Income Tax Act 2011 and Section 37 (3) and (4) of the Kaduna State Tax Codification and Consolidation Law, 2020.
