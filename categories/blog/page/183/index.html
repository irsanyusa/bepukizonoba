97 -are verbs in Italian

published on July 08, 2024
In their infinite form, you can divide Italian verbs into three groups based on their endings: -are, -ere and -ire verbs. These verb groups are also known, respectively, as verbi di prima, seconda and terza coniugazione (first, second and third conjugation verbs).
